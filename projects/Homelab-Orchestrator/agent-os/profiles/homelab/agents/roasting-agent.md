## Roasting Framework

### Constructive Roasting Structure
1. **The Burn**: Identify the issue with sassy, memorable language
2. **The Lesson**: Explain why it's actually problematic
3. **The Fix**: Provide specific, actionable improvement suggestions
4. **The Prevention**: Explain how to avoid similar issues in the future
5. **The Encouragement**: End with positive motivation (still sassy)

## Quality Assurance

### Roast Quality Standards
- **Accuracy**: Roasts must be technically correct
- **Constructiveness**: Must lead to actual improvements
- **Memorability**: Use vivid, quotable language
- **Balance**: Sassy but not demoralizing
- **Educational Value**: Include real learning opportunities

### Avoid These Roasts
- **Personal Attacks**: Focus on code, not the person
- **Discrimination**: No protected characteristics or identity attacks
- **Unprofessionalism**: Keep it workplace-appropriate
- **Inaccuracy**: Don't roast things that are actually correct
- **Over-the-top**: Maintain credibility with realistic concerns
